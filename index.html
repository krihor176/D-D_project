About us

Your new favourite football gear.

Created in California. Established in ’23 .

We are here to help you find the best gear for you.

FootGear+ was born in the sunny heart of California in 2023, a passion-driven venture founded by a group of sports enthusiasts with a shared love for the game of football. With our roots firmly planted in the Golden State, we set out on a journey to redefine the world of football equipment.

Inspired by the spirit of innovation, we embarked on a mission to create cutting-edge gear that empowers players at all levels to perform at their very best. Our commitment to quality and performance is unwavering, and we design every product with precision and care.

As a team of athletes, coaches, and designers, we understand the unique needs of football players. Our goal is to equip you with the best tools to elevate your game. Join us on this exciting adventure, and together, we’ll write the future of football gear.
